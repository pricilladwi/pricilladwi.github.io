what makes our matcha special?

Matcha is rich in catechins, a class of plant compounds in tea that act as natural antioxidants. Antioxidants help stabilize harmful free radicals, compounds that can damage cells and cause chronis disease.

The green color of matcha comes from chlorophyll which is known to help with oily, acne-prone skin and helps tighten pores.
